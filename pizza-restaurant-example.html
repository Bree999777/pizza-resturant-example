Our Story

John Henry's Pizza was opened in 1995 by John Henry Donovan with their pizzas being developed over past generations as it is a family-run Italian restaurant located in the heart of Cork City. They have a strong reputation in Cork for their homemade wood fired Pizzas and diverse menu selection.

We like to stand out by making a pizza with high-quality products, but with prices accessible to all. What distinguishes our pizza is also the use of mother yeast and a slow leavening, at least 72 hours, in a controlled environment, this process together with the flours we use makes our dough highly digestible.
